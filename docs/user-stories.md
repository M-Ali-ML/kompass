# Kompass App User Stories

---

### A. Smart Date, Budget & Accommodation Optimization

* **User Story 1:** As a flexible traveler, I want the app to identify the main, shoulder, and off-seasons for my destination, so that I can find dates that are less crowded and more budget-friendly.
* **User Story 2:** As a budget-conscious traveler, I want to provide a broad timeframe (e.g., 10 to 15 days in September) and have the app recommend the exact best dates to fly based on live prices from Skyscanner or Google Flights.
* **Acceptance Criteria:** The app queries live flight data and returns the cheapest date combinations within the user's defined window.

* **User Story 3 (Comprehensive Budgeting):** As a thorough planner, I want the app to estimate and include accommodation costs alongside transportation costs, so that I can see accurate subtotals and a grand total for my entire trip.
* **User Story 4 (Hotel Availability):** As a traveler with specific tastes, I want the app to check the availability of my favorite or preferred hotels for different date windows, so that I don't book flights for dates when my ideal accommodation is sold out.

---

### B. Intelligent Transportation Logistics

* **User Story 5:** As a traveler who values convenience, I want to set parameters for my travel (e.g., direct flights only, acceptable journey length, morning/night flights), so that I avoid inconvenient routes like flights with three layovers.
* **User Story 6:** As a trip planner, I want the app to automatically synchronize my connecting travel modes and provide specific routing notes (e.g., "Fly to Athens and take the first ferry out to Milos"), so that I have a foolproof logistical plan immediately after landing.
* **Acceptance Criteria:** The generated itinerary must include specific, logically aligned departure and arrival times for multi-modal connections.

---

### C. Scenario Comparison & Scoring

* **User Story 7:** As an analytical planner, I want to view multiple travel scenarios (e.g., different weeks or months) side-by-side in a comparative table, so that I can easily weigh the differences in cost, travel flow, and duration.
* **User Story 8:** As a traveler who balances budget with comfort, I want the app to calculate an "overall stress level" or convenience score for each itinerary, taking into account flight times and difficult connections (like overnight ferries), so that I can make a holistic decision.

---

### D. Activity & Destination Discovery

* **User Story 9:** As an explorer unfamiliar with a new location, I want the app to ask me questions to determine the "vibe" I am looking for, so that it can provide tailored recommendations.
* **User Story 10:** As an independent traveler, I want the ability to manually override activity suggestions or tell the app exactly what I plan to do, so that it focuses on logistical heavy-lifting rather than spoon-feeding me day-to-day sightseeing.

---

### E. User Interface & Output Formats

* **User Story 11:** As a user reviewing my trip, I want to see a high-level visual summary of the itinerary, so that I can quickly grasp the overall flow of my travel.
* **User Story 12:** As a detail-oriented planner, I want the option to view detailed text and tabular breakdowns of my itinerary, so that I can thoroughly understand the specific logistical details.

---

### F. Session Management & Memory

* **User Story 13:** As an iterative planner, I want to be able to provide feedback on the generated itinerary and have the app regenerate the response, so that I am not forced to rely on a single, perfect initial output.
* **User Story 14:** As a busy user, I want the app to save my specific trip conversations, so that I can leave the app and return another day to pick up exactly where I left off.
* **User Story 15:** As a frequent user, I want the app to remember my baseline likes and dislikes globally, so that when I start a new trip conversation, it automatically applies my previous preferences.

---

### G. Autonomy & Manual Labor Reduction

* **User Story 16:** As a traveler overwhelmed by manual planning, I want the app to act with a high degree of autonomy (8-9 out of 10) to generate a complete draft itinerary from a high-level prompt, so that I am saved the manual labor of cross-referencing multiple booking sites.
